Viewpoint School believes the primary responsibility for financing a childs education rests upon the family. After a family assesses all of its resources, the School encourages them to apply for financial aid to make up any difference between the cost of education and the familys contribution.
More information about our Financial Aid program can be found on our website at www.viewpoint.org/admission/finaid under Financial Aid. The application deadline is January 15, and families must use the SSS/PFS online application service found on our website.
